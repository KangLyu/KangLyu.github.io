Publications & Preprints

1. Sharp bound for embedded eigenvalues into the essential spectrum of one dimensional discrete Schrödinger operators (with Wencai Liu). In preparation.

2. One dimensional discrete Schrödinger operators with resonant embedded eigenvalues (with Wencai Liu). Submitted. PDF

We study eigenvalues of the Dirac operator with canonical form where p and q are real functions. Under the assumption that limsup x→ ∞ x√p²(x)+q²(x)<∞, the essential spectrum of Lp,q is (-∞, ∞). We prove that Lp,q has no eigenvalues if limsup x→ ∞ x√p²(x)+q²(x)<1/2. Given any A≥ 1/2 and any λ∈ ℝ, we construct functions p and q such that limsup x→ ∞ x√p²(x)+q²(x)=A and λ is an eigenvalue of the corresponding Dirac operator Lp,q . We also construct functions p and q so that the corresponding Dirac operator Lp,q has any prescribed set (finitely or countably many) of eigenvalues.
